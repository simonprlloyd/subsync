How It Works
Set it once.
It runs itself.

SubSync isn't project management software. It's a coordination engine that texts your subs so you don't have to.

01
🏗️
Add Your Job

Enter the job address, add your trades in order, set approximate start dates. Takes 10 minutes total. Works on your phone.

02

Set The Sequence

Tell SubSync the order — framing before electrical, rough-in before drywall. It handles the logic automatically from there.

When the previous trade confirms done, the next sub automatically gets a text with the address, start date, and what to expect. No app needed on their end.
